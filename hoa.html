Owner Responsibilities

  • • Maintain ALL exterior surfaces including roof, paint, windows
  • • Keep rear yard landscaped and irrigated
  • • Maintain drainage devices - cannot alter or block
  • • Share fence maintenance 50/50 with neighbors
  • • HOA handles common area, slopes, driveways

Drainage (IMPORTANT!)

  • ✗ Owner responsible for drainage on their lot
  • ✗ Cannot block existing drainage paths
  • ✗ Must maintain earth berms at top of slopes
  • ✗ If you add patio/improvements that block drainage, YOU must provide alternative drainage

Use Restrictions

  • • Residential only - no business/commercial use
  • • No temporary structures (tents, shacks)
  • • Garage doors must stay closed except entering/exiting
  • • Pets allowed but can't be "obnoxious" to neighbors
  • • All pets must be leashed outside unit

Pools/Spas in Backyard

  • ✓ Allowed without architectural approval
  • • Must get building permit first
  • • Must carry $300K liability insurance during construction
  • • Construction only 7am-6pm Mon-Sat
  • • Max 6 feet above ground level

Leasing Rules

Renting is allowed but: must rent entire unit (no room rentals), all leases must be in writing, tenant must follow all HOA rules, no short-term/Airbnb rentals (no "transient or hotel purposes").

Insurance (IMPORTANT!)

Per CC&Rs Article VIII: HOA master policy covers common areas and community facilities ONLY. It does NOT cover your building.

  • Julia must insure: Entire building structure (roof, walls, windows, interior)
  • HOA insures: Pool, courts, driveways, common landscaping only
  • Policy type: Standard homeowner's insurance (HO-3) - this is NOT a walls-in condo situation
