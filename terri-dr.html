⚠️ CRITICAL: Landslide History on Terri Drive

1987 - During Construction

Landslide occurred during original grading of American Beauty Classics in the vicinity of Terri Drive.

February 2019 - MAJOR Landslide Event

  • Weekend storm caused landslide between Terri Drive (American Beauty Classics) and Trestles Drive
  • Cracks in backyards became an 8-foot-deep hole that consumed three backyards
  • Two homes on Terri Drive were yellow-tagged
  • Total 5 homes received yellow tags across both communities
  • 40,000 sq ft of plastic sheeting + 250,000 lbs of gravel used in emergency response
  • Officials worked 24 hours/day to protect property

Aftermath

  • Williams Homes (Trestles developer) commenced repairs in late 2019/2020
  • At least one Terri Drive resident filed lawsuit over damage
  • Questions raised about why development was permitted given known slide history
  • The listing for 26921 Terri Dr does NOT mention any landslide history

🚨 Questions Julia MUST Ask

  1. Was THIS specific property affected by the 2019 landslide?
  2. Were there any yellow tags, red tags, or structural reports on this property?
  3. Has there been any ground movement, cracking, or settlement?
  4. Are there any pending or resolved lawsuits related to the landslide?
  5. What is the current status of slope stabilization?
  6. Is the property covered by any geological hazard zone on the NHD report?
