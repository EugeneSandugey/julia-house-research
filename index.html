AB-968 Flipper Disclosure Law (California)

Under California Civil Code 1102.6h (effective July 1, 2024), sellers who sell within 18 months of purchase MUST disclose:

  • Complete list of all work performed (additions, modifications, repairs)
  • Names and contact info for ALL contractors (work over $500)
  • Copies of ALL permits obtained

Request these disclosures! If they can't provide them, you may have grounds for rescission.
