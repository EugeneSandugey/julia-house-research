The R-22 Problem

R-22 refrigerant was phased out January 1, 2020 due to ozone depletion.

  • ✗ No new R-22 can be manufactured or imported
  • ✗ Only recycled R-22 available (limited supply)
  • ✗ Price: $50-100 per pound (was $10-20)

When A/C needs major repair: Full system replacement required (can't just swap the outdoor unit - evaporator coil inside is also R-22 specific)

Replacement Would Include:

  • 1. New condenser (outside unit)
  • 2. New evaporator coil (attic)
  • 3. Usually furnace too (same labor, warranty)
  • Budget: $8,000 - $12,000
